Sketch 005 — Task Pick-Rank (per card, scoped to its cell)

Each card carries a 0–4 strip (1 = do first … 4; – = clear). Numbers unique within the cell; ranked cards sort to the top (1→4), then unranked by due date. Shown: one cell (High · Backlog) with 4 ranked + 2 unranked cards.

Variant A — strip in the card footer + corner number
The 0–4 strip lives in the footer beside the due-date (where the status text used to be). Ranked cards get a left-edge accent + a round corner number. Compact, doesn't add height.

Variant B — top strip + left number rail
A thin 0–4 strip sits at the top of the card; ranked cards show a bold number on a colored left rail (big, scannable). More prominent, costs a little card height/width.
